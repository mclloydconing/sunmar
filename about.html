Our Company

SUNMARExpress Global Services Inc. short for “SUNMAREXPRESS” is a Franchising company with a concept of (BOSS) or Business One Stop Shop. We are engaged in products and services such as E-Loading, E-Games, Satellite Cable Load, Bills Payment, Airlines Ticketing, Bus, Ferry & Hotel Booking, Travel & Tours, Remittance, Courier Services, ATM Withdrawal, Insurance and Medical Health Discount Card etc.

SUNMarExpress is committed to bring its full potential knowledge, skills and attitude to grow and firmly continue exploring innovative ways in terms of its products and services. And the company serves as the root of success of clients, staffs and business partner making an impact to the market.

SUNMarExpress expanded the horizon for business, the Company sees that it will not cease by failures and burdens, but it will always be in continuous search for new Products, Services and Technology with the QUALITY of Service that provides in every Filipinos.
